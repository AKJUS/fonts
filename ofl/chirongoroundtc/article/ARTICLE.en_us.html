Chiron GoRound TC is a rounded Traditional Chinese sans typeface based on Chiron Hei HK and Adobe's Source Han Sans (a.k.a. Google's Noto Sans CJK). The font aims at providing a modern, region-agnostic glyph set adopting the "modern" glyph style that is similar to the prevailing, usually commercial, typefaces in the Traditional Chinese regions.

To contribute, see github.com/chiron-fonts/chiron-go-round-tc.
