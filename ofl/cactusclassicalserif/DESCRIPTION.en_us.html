"Cactus Classical Serif" is an open source font suitable for Traditional Chinese environments. The main producer is Tian Haidong, and Yuwen Manyue participates in the production and maintenance. It is based on the "Hong Kong Folk Character Collection Heritage Glyph Version" font developed by Henry Chan and the character images registered on Glyphwiki, and is based on the "Yuanyang Mingti" developed by But Ko based on the "Siyuan Font" The fonts are supplemented by Western and other characters, with partial reference to inherited glyphs, including the recommended shapes of the standardization documents for inherited glyphs, and other common inherited glyphs. The glyphs are modified to traditional Chinese shapes, and some missing Chinese characters are made up.
